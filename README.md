A modern food discovery platform that helps you find the perfect meal based on your mood, location, and preferences.

## About

FindUrFood is a vibeathon project designed to revolutionize how people discover restaurants and food options. Instead of endless scrolling through generic restaurant listings, FindUrFood matches you with dining experiences that fit your current vibe.

## Features

- **Vibe-Based Matching**: Find restaurants based on your mood and preferences
- **Location-Aware**: Discover great food options near you
- **Smart Recommendations**: AI-powered suggestions tailored to your taste
- **Community-Driven**: User reviews and ratings to help you decide
- **Real-Time Updates**: Live information on wait times, availability, and specials
